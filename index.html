Common Questions

Got questions?
We've got answers

Will I trip on mushrooms?

Nope. Our coffee contains Lion's Mane, not the "magic" kind. It's 100% legal and functional — designed to boost brain power, not make you talk to your plants. You'll get clarity, not hallucinations.

How much caffeine are we talkin'?

Each serving has 89mg of caffeine — that's less than a standard cup (which averages 95–120mg). It's enough to energize you, but with L-theanine, MCTs, and Lion's Mane, you get smooth focus without the crash, jitters, or 3pm regrets.

Is this better than regular coffee?

In our humble opinion, yes. Regular coffee gives you a spike and a slump. Ours gives you focus, clean energy, gut support, and mood balance — thanks to nootropics, prebiotics, and adaptogens. Plus, no crash = no brainer.

What's actually in this stuff?

Instant Arabica Coffee, Lion's Mane Mushroom, L-Theanine + L-Carnitine, MCT Oil, Prebiotic Fiber (Dextrin, Green Banana, Acacia), Turmeric + Black Pepper, Lucuma, Cinnamon, Monk Fruit, and Natural Flavors. All clean. All functional. No weird fillers or fake sweeteners.

Will it break my fast?

Not at all. Each serving has just 16 calories, 0.2g sugar, and 1.2g fiber. It's fasting-friendly and even helps support digestion. Stay focused, stay fasted.

What does it taste like?

Like real coffee — because it is. Smooth, rich, and slightly earthy with a hint of cinnamon and turmeric. No mushroom flavor. No weird aftertaste. Just a cup that tastes as good as it makes you feel.

Can I drink it every day?

Absolutely. It's designed for daily performance. Morning rituals, pre-workout, or mid-afternoon reset — you're good to go. If you're pregnant, breastfeeding, or on meds, just check with your doc first.

How do I make it?

Add 1 tsp to hot water. Stir. Sip. That's it. Want to froth it up with oat milk? Go for it. Iced? Totally. Add your ritual, your way.

Is it vegan & gluten-free?

Yes and yes. It's vegan, gluten-free, dairy-free, soy-free, and keto-friendly. No nonsense. Just functional ingredients your body loves.

Where do you ship & how fast?

We ship to Australia (3–5 business days), the USA (7–14 business days), and worldwide (usually 7–21 days depending on country).

What if I hate it?

We doubt it. But if you're not feeling the magic, no stress. We've got a 30-day money-back guarantee. No hoops. Just shoot us a message — love it or get a full refund.
