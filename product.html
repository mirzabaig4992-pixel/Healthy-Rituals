How to Brew
  1. Mix. Stir 5g (≈ 1 heaped tsp) into 250 mL of hot or cold water.
  2. Sip. Up to twice daily — morning, mid-afternoon, or pre-workout.
  3. Reseal. Store away from direct sunlight to keep it fresh.
